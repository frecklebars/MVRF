Sweatshirt!


Ever wanted more songs but you can't really be bothered to find some? Why not make some yourself! But without actually making them!

Sweatshirt is a markov-chain AI that learns how to compose based on other songs. My default dataset consists of songs from Kanye West, Earl Sweatshirt and Tyler the Creator. I'll add more (planning on adding Kero Kero and Death Grips to the combo). You can also (not yet though) just type a bunch of songs you like and it'll learn from those.

Still work in progress.

Text Color Predictor


It's a neural network I managed to write from scratch to better understand what goes on inside one.


It learns from a bunch of random colors if text is visible better in black or white on different colored backgrounds.


It was fun to make and I learned a bunch.
